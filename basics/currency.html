The currencies

There are three types of obtainable currencies in Altos Wars: EXP, gold and dark psyche. In this document we will be outlining ways to obtain these currencies, what you can buy with them and the conversion rates for trading one currency for another.

EXP

EXP is the least valuable currency in Altos Wars, however this does not make it the least important currency. EXP is obtained simply by killing zombies. For every zombie you kill, one EXP point is gained. You can view how much EXP you currently possess by checking out the scoreboard to the right (as shown in the figure above). Is is important to note that EXP in this case DOES NOT refer to exp in minecraft which you use for enchantments in DEFAULT minecraft. The exp bar in Altos Wars is used to count how many seconds the game has been running for. The current level displayed on the exp bar represents how many seconds the game has been running for. This is useful when you know all the timings of the mob spawns.

EXP can be used to buy a wide range of items such as the core breaker or something simpler such as steak. Check out the EXP shop items document to view the full list of all items. EXP is also the only currency that you can purchase enchantments with. To see the full list of enchantments obtainable via EXP, check out the document on enchantment shop enchantments. You can also convert 20EXP into a single point of dark psyche.

Gold

Gold is obtained by killing other players. For every player kill you will obtain one gold. Unlike other currencies, gold can be lost. For every death, you lose one gold, however if you have zero gold, you will lose none. This is important to consider when saving up gold to buy particular gold items. You can view gold by holding tab. Gold is displayed between the player name and your connection status (as seen in the figure above). Gold can be converted to 5EXP as shown in the figure below. This is sometimes a good idea if you know you are going to die. Gold is used to buy some of the most important items in the game such as the spawn blockers and the Core Breaker 2. To view the full list of obtainable gold items check out the gold shop items document.

Dark psyche

Unlike the currencies listed above, obtaining dark psyche is much more complicated and requires multiple steps. One way to acquire dark psyche is by simply converting 20EXP to dark psyche in the dark psyche shop, however this is inefficient as that EXP could be used for enchantments. The primary method of achieving dark psyche involves killing a mob called the dark spirits. These are basically spiders with several buffs and spawn on both sides of the map at regular intervals. The dark spirits drop an item called dark essence. Dark essence is basically just a block of coal. Dark essence can be converted to dark psyche by placing it on the nether brick inside the underground layer of the ancient tree, as shown in the figure below. Placing a single block of dark essence on the nether brick converts it dark psyche and destroys that block.

To find out more information about dark spirits, check out the document on dark spirits . To find out more about the ancient tree, check out the document on the ancient tree. Now that we've discussed how to obtain this unique currency, it's time to discuss what dark psyche is used for. Dark psyche is used to purchase some of the most interesting items in the game called dark pscyhe items. These are not normal items; dark psyche items have their own unique properties. Most of the time these properties are positive such as extra damage, but some items offer benefits at a cost. Dark psyche items play a large role in Altos Wars, sometimes deciding who wins or not. To see the full list of dark psyche items, check out the dark psyche shop items document.
