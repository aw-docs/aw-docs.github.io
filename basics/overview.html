Brief overview of the game

Now that you know how to setup the game, you are finally ready to begin playing. However, before you dive in,you first must know some rudimentary knowledge of how the game works. This document will give you the minimum knowledge required to play the game, however there is still a lot more left to learn.

The main premise

Two teams, the Hunters and the Altos who have invaded the Hunter's land, must battle each other out in a 1v1 pvp battle, while attempting to destroy each other's cores to ultimately win the game. Both players are assisted with unique items that they can buy along the way at various stores. In addition to this, both players play as one of the various classes which all have their unique perks and gimmicks giving them unique playstyles. The first person that can finally kill off a player without them respawning is crowned victor. Players will continually respawn while their core is still present. You may then be wondering, what is this core, and how do I destroy it?

The core

The core is simply two obsidian blocks stacked on top of each other as seen in the figure above. It is located in the respective player's base right in front of their spawn pad. Once the player's core is fully destroyed by breaking both obsidian blocks, that player will no longer be able to respawn. Once that player has died, the game ends and the opposing player wins. While the core is still alive, the player will continue to respawn in their spawn pad, however there is a short delay of a few seconds. There are only two items in the game that can destroy the core: a diamond pickaxe called the Core Breaker and an enchanted diamond pickaxe called the Core Breaker 2. The Core Breaker can be purchased at the EXP shop while the Core Breaker 2 can be purchased at the gold shop. We will now go over a brief overview of the currencies and items in the game. For further information about the core, check out the document on the bases.

The currencies, items and shops

As previously discussed, you will either need EXP or gold to purchase either of the core breakers so that you can defeat your enemy. EXP is simply acquired by killing zombies. For every zombie you kill you gain exactly one EXP. Gold is simply acquired by killing the other player. Every time you kill the other player you gain one gold. However, if you die you lose one gold . Of course, if you have 0 gold you will not lose any. But, keep this in mind when saving up for Core Breaker 2 which costs 5 gold. The core breaker in the EXP shop costs only 13exp. Each team has their own EXP and gold shops, however any team can buy items from any shop. If you want to find out more about all the EXP shop items available check out the document on EXP shop items . If you want to find out more about all the gold shop items available check out the document on gold shop items . Gold and EXP aren't the only currencies in the game; there is also dark psyche which is a currency a bit more tricky to explain. Dark psyche plays an extremely important role in the game, so it's highly suggested that you read the full document on the currencies . It is also important to understand the mobs, as you obtain most currencies by killing mobs in the game.
